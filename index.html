Summary

"I Told Sunset About You (known in Thai as แปลรักฉันด้วยใจเธอ; RTGS: Plae Rak Chan Duai Chai Thoe, lit. "translate my love with your heart") is a 2020 Thai romantic drama coming-of-age streaming television series by Nadao Bangkok. It stars Putthipong "Billkin" Assaratanakul and Krit "PP" Amnuaydechkorn, and is being released in two five-episode parts. Part 1, directed by Naruebet Kuno, tells the story of the relationship between two secondary schoolboys living in Phuket as they prepare for university admissions, while Part 2, to be directed by Tossaphon Riantong, will pick up after their entry into university."

"Thailand's #1 worldwide trending series of the year, showing the relationship between two boys and their feelings and social problems."
